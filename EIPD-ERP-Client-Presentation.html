From work order to finished
goods — fully traceable.

Work Orders

6 status filters

Equipment status (Running / Idle / Maintenance) with live OEE %, key parameters, and operator notes — for true shop-floor visibility.

Quality Control

Finished Goods

WO + QC linked

Versioned SOPs

Traceability promise

Every finished good can be traced back to its raw material batch, work order, shift, machine, and QC inspector — in under 30 seconds.

Pillar 02 · Inventory & Procurement

Stock you can trust.
POs that close themselves.

Inventory Register

Raw material stock with native units (kg / pcs / L / sets / m), reorder & minimum levels, supplier linkage, and one-click CSV export for audits.

Reorder alerts

Purchase Orders

Supplier procurement against the vendor master. Status pipeline: Pending Approval → Raised → In Transit → Delivered → Cancelled.

5-stage pipeline

GRN — Goods Receipt

Receipt against PO with received / accepted / rejected quantities, vehicle details, inspector name, and supplier bill references. Discrepancies flagged instantly.

Variance tracking

Reorder levels are set per material. The dashboard warns the moment stock falls below minimum, before the line stops.

Three-way match — PO, GRN, and Inward Bill cross-referenced automatically. Discrepancies surface before payment, not after.

Pillar 03 · Sales & Finance

Quote to cash,
in one continuous thread.

Sales Orders

Customer & DISCOM accounts, buyer master linkage, GST fields, shipping address, and direct Work Order linking.

Quotations

Multi-item quotes with RFQ reference. Submission modes: Email · Portal · Courier · Hand · WhatsApp · Other. Follow-up tracking with status: Draft → Submitted → Under Review → Won / Lost.

Dispatch

Delivery challans with vehicle, transporter, and LR / docket. Real-time status: In Transit → Delivered.

Invoices

GST billing with line items, payment terms (Net 30 / 45 / 60 / Immediate), shipping-vs-billing address toggle. Overdue flagged automatically.

Inward Bills

Supplier bills linked to POs. GST % slabs (0/5/12/18/28), full payment lifecycle tracking from receipt to settlement.

Cost Control

Track capital, equipment, legal/permit, fixed, variable, and project benefit budgets. Monthly and yearly views with allocation transparency.
